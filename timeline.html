The Age of Gods

6000 PS - 3000 PS

The time of legends and folklore when the Gods ruled Hexus. During this time each race would see the cruelty of the gods and overthrow them in turn. From Suren the language of Alerian (derived from the word Alert) is developed and sailers are sent across Hexus, in a means to understand eachother and warn the other races should the gods return....

-2000 AS

The Dawn of the Diamond kings

The First Diamond King unites the waring Seraphim race under a single country, Gurog.

-1000 AS -160 A

The Seperation of the Seraphim

Growing unrest and cultural differences causes the Seraphim to split into four sects. Sinahara, Shagar, Meraxus and Seraph

-160 AS

The Fall of the Diamond Kingdom

The Last of the Diamond Kings, known as the Glass Woman is killed during an uprising by Sect Meraxus, Their line ended and kingdom disolved.
