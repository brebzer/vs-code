Welcome to World of Hexus

The legacys of Hexus is an upcoming fantasy series set in a magical and immersive world. Explore the locations, characters, and races of this unique setting.


Follow the journey of "The Sea Runner" William Anron as he is drawn into a conflict that will change the fate of Hexus forever.
All the while his son Borlock is caught between two worlds, and must find his own path.

Discover the rich lore and intricate history that will lead to the beginning of our story.

Stay tuned for more updates on Book 1 "The Sea Runner's call"!
